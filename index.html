About me

I am a PhD student in Cryptography at IBM Research Zurich and the University of Potsdam since November 2024. I am advised by Dr. Vadim Lyubashevsky and Prof. Dr. Anja Lehmann.

Previously, I completed a MSc in Computer Science at ETH Zurich and, before that, a Double Bachelor Degree in Mathematics and Computer Science at the University of Granada.
